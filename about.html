1047 Games

1047 Games was founded by Stanford graduates Ian Proulx and Nicholas Bagamian as a dorm-room project.

Their hit game, Splitgate, has been downloaded over 10 million times.

With a team of over 100 members, they are now developing Splitgate 2, a AAA shooter that aims to redefine competitive FPS with portals and innovative gameplay.
